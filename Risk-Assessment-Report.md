# Risk Assessment Report

### Purpose
The purpose of this assessment is to identify, evaluate, and manage information security risks affecting DesertTech Solutions LLC.

### Scope
This assessment covers the following operational domains:
* HR
* Finance
* IT
* Sales
* Operations

Assets evaluated include company laptops, internal servers, enterprise cloud services, employee files, and sensitive customer databases.

### Methodology
The risk assessment followed structured industry steps:
1. Identify organizational assets
2. Identify relevant threats
3. Uncover existing vulnerabilities
4. Assess probability/likelihood of occurrence
5. Assess business impact severity
6. Calculate unified risk level scores
7. Recommend formal mitigation/treatment plans

*Likelihood and Impact metrics are rated standardly on a scale from 1 to 5.*

### Risk Rating Matrix
| Score | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1–5 | Low |
| 6–10 | Medium |
| 11–15 | High |
| 16–25 | Critical |

### Top Five Risks
1. **Phishing Attack**
   * Likelihood: 5 | Impact: 4 | Risk Score: 20 (Critical)
   * *Treatment:* Mandatory employee awareness training, edge email filtering implementation, and company-wide Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
2. **Ransomware**
   * Likelihood: 4 | Impact: 5 | Risk Score: 20 (Critical)
   * *Treatment:* Isolated offline backups retention, advanced endpoint protection deployment, and strict patch management workflows.
3. **Weak Passwords**
   * Likelihood: 4 | Impact: 4 | Risk Score: 16 (Critical)
   * *Treatment:* Hardened corporate password policy, automated MFA enforcement, and deployment of centralized enterprise password managers.
4. **Cloud Misconfiguration**
   * Likelihood: 3 | Impact: 5 | Risk Score: 15 (High)
   * *Treatment:* Scheduled infrastructure configuration reviews, fine-grained access control models, and active security monitoring.
5. **Insider Threat**
   * Likelihood: 3 | Impact: 5 | Risk Score: 15 (High)
   * *Treatment:* Strict enforcement of least privilege principles, user behavior monitoring, and comprehensive background check screenings.

### Conclusion
DesertTech Solutions LLC must prioritize remediating phishing attacks, ransomware risks, weak credential policies, cloud safety configurations, and internal insider threats. By implementing these recommended administrative and technical controls, the organization will significantly reduce its active attack surface and enhance its overall security posture.

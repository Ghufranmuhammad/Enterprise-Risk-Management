# Information Security Objectives

## Objective 1
Reduce high-risk vulnerabilities by 50% within the next 12 months.

## Objective 2
Achieve 100% completion of annual security awareness training by all employees.

## Objective 3
Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all employees within six months.

## Objective 4
Complete quarterly risk assessments for all critical business systems.

## Objective 5
Reduce phishing email click rates by 40% within one year through awareness training.
